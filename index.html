About

Cricketing is a platform where the users can buy stocks of cricket players. The stocks of players are bought using coins given to them during signup. The stocks are valid for one day after which the user will get back their original coin total. They will receive dividends for their stocks based on the players performance during a match. They can use the dividends to claim vouchers for cashbacks and discounts. The top earners for each week will be given cash bonuses
