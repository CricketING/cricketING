FAQs

Can I pick the same player twice?

Yes, you can pick the same player as many times as you want within the available coins for any matchday.

When can I select the players?

You can select the players on a matchday just before the game starts (i.e before the first ball is bowled).

Should I invest money to use the app?

You need not invest any money,on the flipside you can earn money through cashbacks and getting good ranks on the leaderboard.

Can I earn coins other than those given to me during signup?

Yes, you can earn coins by our referral system where you will be given 500 coins for each referral.You can also get extra coins by participating in quizzes or by watching ads.

Will all the players be priced equally?

No, The players will be divided into 3 categories (Tier 1,2 and 3) based on their past perfonances and experience
